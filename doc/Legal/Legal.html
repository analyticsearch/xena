Licensing

Xena is released under the General Public License (GPL), an open source license, that allows for unrestricted distribution and modification of the Xena application (so long as any further modifications are also distributed under the GPL).

This means it is possible for other agencies (and others) to customise Xena to suite their own purposes. Of course the National Archives is interested in the use others make of Xena and welcomes the opportunity of others making their contributions available to the National Archives for inclusion in the 'main' distribution.
