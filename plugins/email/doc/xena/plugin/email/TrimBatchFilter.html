xena.plugin.email
Class TrimBatchFilter

public class TrimBatchFilter
extends xena.kernel.BatchFilter
Filters out the Trim mail attachments. When normalising a batch of files exported from TRIM, some of the files are not stand-alone but are actually attachments for the email .mbx files. We filter these out of consideration because they will be bundled into the Xena email file.
